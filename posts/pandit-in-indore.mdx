### 2. Satyanarayan Katha: For Family Prosperity and Gratitude

The **Satyanarayan Katha** is one of the most beloved and powerful pujas performed in Hindu households. It is an expression of gratitude to Lord Vishnu (as Lord Satyanarayan) and is performed to seek his blessings for success, health, and fulfillment of desires.

**When should you perform Satyanarayan Katha?**
* On auspicious days like Purnima (Full Moon) or Ekadashi.
* After a major life event (new job, promotion, child's birth).
* For birthdays, anniversaries, or before exams.
* Anytime you wish to fill your home with positive vibrations.

Pandit Ji not only recites the Katha but also explains the stories and their moral significance, engaging the entire family in the process.

Do you feel a sense of imbalance, stress, or frequent obstacles at home or in your office? This could be due to Vastu doshas (architectural flaws). A **Vastu Shanti Puja** is a powerful remedy that harmonizes the five elements (Earth, Water, Fire, Air, Space) within your property.
